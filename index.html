Why ERPB Exists

Educational institutions should be places of learning, growth, safety, and dignity. Unfortunately, students, teachers, and staff sometimes face issues that go unnoticed or unreported. ERPB exists to promote awareness, encourage responsible reporting, and help communities understand educational rights and responsibilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ERPB?
ERPB is an independent awareness and reporting platform focused on educational rights, transparency, and community awareness.
Is ERPB a government organization?
No. ERPB is not affiliated with any government authority, law enforcement agency, educational institution, or regulatory body.
Can reports be submitted anonymously?
The platform is designed to support privacy-conscious reporting options where applicable.
Are reports verified facts?
No. Reports are user-submitted allegations and should not automatically be considered verified information.
Who can use ERPB?
Students, parents, teachers, staff members, and educational communities.

Important Legal Disclaimer
